I am a Software Engineer specialized in Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing.

I am also a professional coffee drinker.

Projects

I’m currently working full-time on a personal project called Open IELTS, a set of freely available test preparation tools that help IELTS test takers reach their goals on their own, without being ripped off by the lucrative couching institutions.

Before this, I worked at LAIX (aka Liulishuo 英语流利说) as a ML/NLP Software Engineer (aka 算法工程师), from May 2018 to May 2019. My major contribution was an English writing platform called 流利写作(Fluent Writing). I enjoyed this period a lot and would highly recommend this company to anyone who might be interested.

I also worked for a short period at Alibaba iDST in early 2018. My work mainly focused on the Named Entity Recognition algorithms of Lazada.com.

My first job was a ML/NLP software engineer at Machine Zone. I spent 2 years here from 2015 to 2017. I contributed and led several projects ranging from language detection, word tokenization, text normalization, machine translation, as well as several data science related projects.

Study

I graduated from Brandeis University with a master’s degree in Computational Linguistics.

I got my bachelor’s degree in Korean Studies at Shandong University. My major has nothing to do with Computer Science or Engineering, but after realizing that my real passion is in language technologies, like machine translation, I had dedicated thousands of hours in the fields of computer science, linguistics, and math, and finally made my way to this industry and became who I am today.
